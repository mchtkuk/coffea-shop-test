Here's timeline of Nelson Mandela's life:

  • 1918 - Born Rolihlahla Mandela at Mvezo in the Transkei

  • 1918 - Attends primary school near Qunu (receives the name ‘Nelson’ from a teacher)

  • 1918 - Father dies. Entrusted to Thembu Regent Jongintaba Dalindyebo at the age of 12 While his autobiography Long Walk to Freedom places Mandela's father’s death in 1927, historical evidence shows it must have been later, most likely 1930.

  • 1918 - Undergoes initiation; Attends Clarkebury Boarding Institute in Engcobo

  • 1918 - Sent to Robben Island

  • 1918 - Attends Healdtown, the Wesleyan College at Fort Beaufort

  • 1918 - Attends the installation of his grandson Mandla as chief of the Mvezo Traditional Council

  • 1918 - His book Nelson Mandela By Himself: The Authorised Book of Quotations is launched

  • 1918 - Passes away at home in Johannesburg
